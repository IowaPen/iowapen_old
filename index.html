IowaPen

Iowa's Outstanding Writing And Pen Enthusiast's Network

A gathering of people interested in fountain pens, mechanical pencils and writing instruments of all kinds.
Calligraphy, penmanship, pen collecting, vintage pen restoration, nib tuning, ink testing/sharing, paper, pen turning, history and more.

Central Iowa chapter Meetings are held from 4:00 - 6:00 pm on the 2nd Sunday of every month in the conference room at:

Café Diem
2005 S. Ankeny Blvd.
Ankeny, Iowa 50023 U.S.A.
